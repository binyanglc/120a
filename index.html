Hey Y'all!


Students often ask me where they can find authentic Chinese practice materials. It can be difficult to find suitable practice materials for intermediate students. In most cases, real-world Chinese materials are too difficult or too artificial. Thus, we have created this website, where intermediate students can find authentic material. I would be glad to hear from you via email at binyanglc@gmail.com if you have any suggestions or if you would like to contribute.
